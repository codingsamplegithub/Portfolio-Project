The Biggest University In Ethiopia

Addis Ababa University (AAU), which was established in 1950
as the University College of Addis Ababa (UCAA), is the oldest and
the largest higher learning and research institution in Ethiopia.
Since its inception, the University has been the leading center in
teaching-learning, research and community services.
